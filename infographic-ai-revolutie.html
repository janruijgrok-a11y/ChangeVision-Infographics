3. Death of the Search Engine

The massive advertising model, fuelled by search behavior, faces an existential threat. Generative AI fundamentally changes how users consume information.

The Old Model: Links & Ads

  • **Query:** User asks question in search engine.
  • **Result:** Links to external websites.
  • **Monetization:** Ads displayed alongside links.
  • **Power:** Control over the information gateway (Google is the gatekeeper).

The New Model: AI & Answers

  • **Query:** User asks question to AI assistant.
  • **Result:** Direct, summarized answer without external links.
  • **Monetization:** Subscriptions and licenses for AI models.
  • **Power:** Control over knowledge itself (AI delivers the final product).

*The shift from 'links' to 'answers' constitutes an existential risk to the $200 billion advertising paradigm.*
